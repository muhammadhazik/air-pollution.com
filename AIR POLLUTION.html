Human-made Sources:

Burning fossil fuels (in vehicles, power plants, factories), agricultural activities, and industrial processes.

Natural Sources:

Volcanic eruptions, dust storms, wildfires, and the release of gases like methane from decomposing organic matter.

HARD SITUATIONS

Particulate Matter (PM): Small particles that can penetrate deep into the lungs and bloodstream, causing respiratory and cardiovascular problems.
