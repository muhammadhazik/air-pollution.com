    2. Water Pollution:

  • Unsafe water causes ~1.2 million deaths/year (Our World in Data).

  • Contaminants: Industrial waste, sewage, agricultural runoff (nitrates, phosphates).

  • Oceans: Estimated 11 million tons of plastic waste enter oceans annually (Pew Research).

    • 3. Soil Pollution:

  • Main Causes: Pesticides, heavy metals, industrial waste.

  • Impact: Affects food safety, biodiversity, and human health.

4. Noise Pollution:

  • Linked to cardiovascular diseases, stress, and sleep disorders.

  • EU report (EEA, 2023): Around 20% of Europeans are exposed to harmful noise levels (primarily traffic).
